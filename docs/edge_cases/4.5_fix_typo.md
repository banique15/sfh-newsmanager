# "Fix a Typo" or Small Edit

## Overview

User says "fix the typo in the second paragraph" or "change 'June 15' to 'June 20'" with a specific, small correction.

This is a targeted update. We identify the article, make the small change, and confirm.

## Why This Approach

Small edits still affect live content, so confirmation ensures the right article and right change.

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### 1. **Identify Article and Change**

**Tool(s) used:** `context_resolution`

**Extract:**
- Which article (from context or explicit reference)
- What to change (specific text)
- What to change it to

### 2. **Confirm the Fix**

**Message:**
> "I'll fix the typo in '[ARTICLE_TITLE]', changing '[old text]' to '[new text]'. Is this correct?"

### 3. **Apply Update**

**Tool(s) used:** `article_crud`

**Follow:** [3.4_update_content.md](../core_tasks/3.4_update_content.md)

## Expected Outcome

Small, targeted update applied after confirmation.
